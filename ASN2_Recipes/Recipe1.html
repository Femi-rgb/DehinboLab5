Recipe 1: Fried Rice

A colorful stir-fried rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables, spices, and seasoned stock.

Servings: 6 Calories: ~400 per serving Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked parboiled rice
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • ½ cup diced liver or chicken (optional)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning cubes to taste
  • Spring onions, sliced (optional)

Preparation

  1. If using liver or chicken, season lightly and stir-fry in a little oil until cooked; set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan, add chopped onions and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Stir in curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt; mix well.
  5. Add the cooked rice and chicken stock in small amounts while stir-frying so the rice absorbs the flavor.
  6. Continue to stir-fry until the rice is hot, well-mixed, and no liquid remains at the bottom.
  7. Fold in the fried liver or chicken pieces and sliced spring onions (if using).
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot.
