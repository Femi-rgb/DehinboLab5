Recipe 5: Yam Porridge (Asaro)

A creamy, savory yam dish cooked in spicy pepper sauce, palm oil, and aromatics.

Servings: 5 Calories: ~420 per serving Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yam, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 cup blended pepper mix (tomatoes, red peppers, onions)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • ½ cup palm oil
  • 1–2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: smoked fish
  • Optional: chopped spinach or ugu leaves
  • Water, enough to cook the yam

Preparation

  1. Place the yam cubes in a pot, add enough water to almost cover them, and season lightly with salt.
  2. Add the blended pepper mix and chopped onion, then bring to a boil.
  3. Stir in palm oil, seasoning cubes, and smoked fish if using, then continue cooking until the yam is soft.
  4. Use a spoon to break some of the yam pieces to thicken the porridge while leaving some chunks whole.
  5. Add chopped spinach or ugu near the end of cooking and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary, then serve hot.
