Recipe 6: Moi-Moi

A smooth steamed bean pudding made from blended peeled beans, peppers, and seasonings.

Servings: 8 Calories: ~220 per serving Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 45–60 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups peeled beans
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 onion
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for blending
  • Optional: boiled eggs, fish, or corned beef

Preparation

  1. Blend the beans, peppers, and onion with water until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Pour the mixture into a bowl and stir in the vegetable oil, seasoning cubes, and salt.
  3. Whisk the batter to incorporate air for a lighter texture.
  4. Pour the mixture into containers or foil bowls and add optional fillings.
  5. Steam for 45–60 minutes until the Moi-Moi is firm and fully cooked.
