Recipe 3: Akara (Bean Cake)

Crispy deep-fried bean fritters made from blended beans, onions, and peppers.

Servings: 6 Calories: ~250 per serving Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups peeled beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper (or to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for blending (very small amount)
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Preparation

  1. Blend the peeled beans, onion, and pepper with as little water as possible to form a thick, smooth batter.
  2. Pour the batter into a bowl and whisk vigorously to incorporate air and lighten the mixture.
  3. Season with salt and stir gently to combine.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan until hot but not smoking.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the oil and fry on medium heat, turning occasionally until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the Akara and drain on paper towels before serving.
