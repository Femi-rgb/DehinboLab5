Recipe 4: Nigerian Meat Pie

A flaky baked pastry stuffed with seasoned minced meat, potatoes, and carrots.

Servings: 10 pies Calories: ~300 per pie Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 30–35 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • About ½ cup cold water
  • 300 g minced beef
  • 2 potatoes, diced small
  • 1 carrot, diced small
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1–2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp flour (for thickening filling)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt, then rub in the cold butter until crumbly.
  2. Add cold water a little at a time and gently bring together into a soft dough; set aside to rest.
  3. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, then add minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add diced potatoes and carrot, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper; cook with a little water until just tender.
  5. Stir in 1 tablespoon of flour to thicken the mixture; let the filling cool completely.
  6. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut into circles or ovals.
  7. Place some filling on one side of each dough circle, fold over, and seal the edges by pressing with a fork.
  8. Arrange pies on a baking tray, brush with beaten egg, and bake at 175°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes or until golden brown.
